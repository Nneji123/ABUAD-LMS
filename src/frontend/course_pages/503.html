Computer Aided Design(CAD)

Computer-aided design (CAD) is the use of computer software to create, modify, analyze, and optimize designs. CAD software is used by architects, engineers, graphic designers, and other professionals to create 2D or 3D models of products, buildings, or other objects. CAD software allows designers to create more accurate and detailed designs than traditional manual drafting methods. CAD models can be easily modified and shared, making the design process more efficient and collaborative.
